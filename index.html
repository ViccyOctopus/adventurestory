Beside a crackling campfire, a motley group of adventurers and mercenaries sat, sharing stories of their exploits.

Men and women of all ages and appearances jumped to their feet, one by one, each tale more outrageous and extreme than the last. Feeling the need to prove myself, I stood abruptly, exclaiming “I have stories that put all of your ventures to shame, sit down ladies and gentleman… and listen.”

Before I could begin to spin my tale, a rustle of cloth caused me to turn my head, where an old, battered man stood before me, a finger pointed in my face.

“Foolish boy, you know nothing of the world, nothing of hardship and survival. You have hardly any scars on you! Cockiness will get you killed out there, so let me give you a piece of advice: your perspective and choices shape how events unfold. These decisions can't be undone, so choose wisely. So go on, go make a name for yourself. Maybe the next time you find yourself at a fire, you won’t have to lie through your teeth about fame and glory.”

In shock and shame, I sat down. The others quickly resumed their story, but I barely heard them. I’d show that silly old man what a real adventurer was…

The next day, I collected my things, stuffed them in a pack and set out into the nearby woods. Forests were full of cute bunnies and gurgling streams… How bad could it be?

DISCLAIMER: Decisions made in the story are final - there is no turning back.
